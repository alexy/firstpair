# Lighthouse Republics Public Preview

*Lighthouse Republics* is a comparative history of the United States and Venice:
two republics briefly alive in the same diplomatic world, and then linked for a
long afterlife through commerce, memory, architecture, politics, and the idea of
republican light.

This public package is a preview, not the complete book. It is designed to show
the cover, the table of contents, and the opening movement of the manuscript.

## Preview Limit

The preview should include only the opening chapter or opening few chapters, up
to 10 percent of the full body text. The table of contents may show the full
book so readers can understand the complete structure.

The source worktrees own the exact preview cutoff and build metadata. This
First Pair folder owns the public delivery package.
